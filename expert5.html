Course Overview

This Digital Marketing course is designed to teach you SEO techniques, manage social media platforms, create content strategies, and run paid ad campaigns. You will master tools like Google Analytics, Facebook Ads Manager, WordPress, and Canva to build strong online brand presence.

Key Learning Outcomes

  • Understand SEO, keyword research, and ranking techniques.
  • Create and manage effective Google Ads and Facebook Ads campaigns.
  • Build engaging social media marketing strategies across platforms.
  • Master content creation and copywriting for better engagement.
  • Analyze marketing performance using Google Analytics and reporting tools.

Career Prospects with This Course

  • Become a Digital Marketer, SEO Specialist, or Content Strategist in leading companies.
  • Manage Paid Ads as a PPC Expert on Google, Facebook, and Instagram.
  • Handle Social Media Marketing campaigns for brands and agencies.
  • Work as a Marketing Analyst, studying trends and optimizing campaigns.
  • Build your own Digital Marketing Agency or work as a freelancer.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Digital Marketing?

Digital Marketing is the promotion of brands using digital channels and technologies to connect with potential customers. It includes SEO, social media marketing, content marketing, email marketing, PPC advertising, and analytics to measure campaign performance.

Do I need prior marketing experience for this course?

No prior marketing experience is required. The course starts with digital marketing fundamentals and gradually builds up to advanced strategies. We provide comprehensive training suitable for beginners and professionals looking to upgrade their skills.

Will I receive a certificate after completing the Digital Marketing course?

Yes, upon successful completion of the course and capstone project, you will receive a certificate from Rohil NextGen that validates your Digital Marketing skills and can be shared with employers.

What kind of projects will I work on during the course?

You'll work on real-world digital marketing projects including SEO optimization, social media campaigns, Google Ads campaigns, content creation, email marketing automation, and a comprehensive capstone project that integrates all the digital marketing skills you've learned.

What tools and platforms will I learn?

You'll learn industry-standard tools including Google Analytics, Google Ads, Facebook Ads Manager, SEO tools (SEMrush/Ahrefs), email marketing platforms (Mailchimp), social media management tools, and various analytics and reporting platforms.

What career support do you provide after course completion?

We provide comprehensive career support including resume building, interview preparation, portfolio development guidance, and connections with our placement partners for job opportunities in digital marketing agencies, corporate marketing departments, and freelance opportunities.
