Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our placement training

What is the duration of the placement training program?

The duration varies based on the course and individual requirements, typically ranging from 4 to 26 weeks.

Do I need prior experience to join the placement training?

No prior experience is required. Our programs are designed for both freshers and experienced professionals.

What is the success rate of placements after training?

We have a high placement success rate of over 85% for students who complete our training programs.

Do you provide interview preparation assistance?

Yes, we provide comprehensive interview preparation including mock interviews, HR round practice, and technical interview guidance.

Are there any job guarantees after completing the training?

While we cannot guarantee employment, we provide 100% placement assistance and have a strong track record of placing our students in reputed companies.

What kind of companies do you have tie-ups with?

We have tie-ups with over 500+ companies across various industries including IT, manufacturing, healthcare, finance, and more.
