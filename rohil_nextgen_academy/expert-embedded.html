Expert Embedded Systems Course Overview

This expert-level Embedded Systems training program is designed for electronics and computer science professionals who want to master advanced embedded technologies. The course covers real-time operating systems (RTOS), firmware development, hardware design, IoT integration, and advanced microcontroller programming. Students will gain hands-on experience building advanced embedded solutions for automotive, industrial automation, consumer electronics, and medical devices.

Advanced Microcontrollers

Master advanced microcontroller architectures.

  • ARM Cortex-M Series
  • STM32 Platform Development
  • ESP32 & IoT Integration
  • Peripheral Interfacing (SPI, I2C, UART, CAN)
  • DMA & Interrupt Management
  • Low-Power Design Techniques

Real-Time Operating Systems (RTOS)

Master RTOS for real-time embedded applications.

  • RTOS Fundamentals & Scheduling
  • FreeRTOS & Zephyr RTOS
  • Task Management & Synchronization
  • Semaphores, Mutex & Queues
  • RTOS Memory Management
  • Real-Time Debugging Techniques

Firmware Development

Master professional firmware development practices.

  • Embedded C & C++ Advanced
  • HAL & LL Drivers
  • Firmware Architecture Design
  • Bootloader Development
  • OTA Updates & Firmware Management
  • Code Quality & Testing

IoT & Wireless Integration

Learn IoT integration for connected embedded systems.

  • Edge Computing for Embedded
