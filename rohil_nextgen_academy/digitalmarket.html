Course Overview

This Digital Marketing course teaches you how to build, manage, and optimize digital campaigns across platforms. Learn SEO, SEM, PPC, content strategy, email marketing, and performance analytics with tools like Google Ads, Meta Business Suite, and more.

Digital marketing is one of the fastest-growing career fields globally. Businesses are investing heavily in online presence, creating massive demand for skilled digital marketing professionals.

Key Learning Outcomes

  • Master On-page and Off-page SEO techniques
  • Run effective campaigns on Google & Meta Ads Manager
  • Analyze traffic using Google Analytics & GA4
  • Build email marketing funnels with Mailchimp
  • Craft impactful content & copy for various platforms
  • Use AI-powered marketing tools (ChatGPT, etc.)

Digital Marketing Syllabus

Week 1-2: Introduction to Digital Marketing

Theory

  • What is Digital Marketing?
  • Traditional vs Digital Marketing
  • Digital Marketing Channels (SEO, SEM, SMM, Email, Affiliate)
  • Sales funnel & customer journey

Practical

  • Set up a personal brand or project
  • Create a Google account and set up a basic blog

Week 3-4: Website Planning & CMS (WordPress)

Theory

  • Website structure and user experience
  • WordPress basics and theme selection
  • Essential plugins for marketing

Practical

  • Install and customize a WordPress website
  • Set up essential marketing plugins
  • Create a landing page for lead generation

Week 5-6: Search Engine Optimization (SEO)

Theory

  • On-page SEO techniques
  • Off-page SEO and link building
  • Technical SEO fundamentals
  • Local SEO strategies

Practical

  • Perform keyword research using tools
  • Optimize website content for SEO
  • Create and implement an SEO strategy

Week 7-8: Content Marketing & Blogging

Theory

  • Content strategy development
  • Blog writing and optimization
  • Content distribution channels

Practical

  • Create a content calendar
  • Write and optimize blog posts
  • Develop content for different platforms

Week 9-10: Social Media Marketing (SMM)

Theory

  • Platform-specific strategies (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter)
  • Content creation for social media
  • Social media advertising basics

Practical

  • Set up and optimize social media profiles
  • Create and schedule social media content
  • Run a small social media ad campaign

Week 11-12: Paid Ads & Google Ads (PPC)

Theory

  • Google Ads platform overview
  • Campaign structure and types
  • Keyword research and bidding strategies

Practical

  • Set up a Google Ads account
  • Create and launch a search campaign
  • Monitor and optimize campaign performance

Week 13-14: Analytics & Reporting

Theory

  • Google Analytics setup and configuration
  • Key performance indicators (KPIs)
  • Data analysis and interpretation

Practical

  • Set up Google Analytics tracking
  • Analyze website traffic and user behavior
  • Create performance reports and dashboards

Week 15-16: Email & Affiliate Marketing

Theory

  • Email marketing strategy and automation
  • List building and segmentation
  • Affiliate marketing fundamentals

Practical

  • Set up an email marketing platform
  • Create and send email campaigns
  • Set up and manage an affiliate program
