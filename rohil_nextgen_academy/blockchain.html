Course Overview

This Blockchain Fundamentals course is designed to teach you decentralized technologies, cryptographic techniques, blockchain consensus mechanisms, and smart contract development using platforms like Ethereum and Hyperledger.

Key Learning Outcomes

  • Understand the core principles of Blockchain and Decentralized Systems.
  • Learn about Cryptography and Hash Functions used in Blockchain.
  • Explore Bitcoin, Ethereum, and other cryptocurrency ecosystems.
  • Develop and Deploy Smart Contracts on Ethereum.
  • Dive into Blockchain Networks, Nodes, Consensus, and Mining.

Career Prospects with This Course

  • Become a Blockchain Developer or Engineer.
  • Work as a Smart Contract Developer in fintech or tech startups.
  • Enter Blockchain Project Management and Consultancy roles.
  • Explore research roles in Blockchain R&D organizations.
  • Launch your own decentralized applications (DApps) and blockchain products.
