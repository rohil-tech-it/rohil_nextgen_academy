Expert VLSI Design Course Overview

This expert-level VLSI Design training program is designed for electronics graduates and professionals who want to master advanced chip design methodologies. The course covers ASIC design flow, RTL synthesis, static timing analysis (STA), physical design, DFT (Design for Testability), and chip verification. Students will gain hands-on experience using industry-standard EDA tools and work on advanced chip design projects for AI processors, automotive electronics, and 5G communication systems.

ASIC Design Flow

Master the complete ASIC design flow from specification to tape-out.

  • Specification & Architecture Design
  • RTL Coding & Simulation
  • Synthesis & Optimization
  • Floor planning & Placement
  • Routing & Physical Verification
  • GDSII Generation & Tape-out

RTL Synthesis & Optimization

Learn advanced RTL synthesis techniques for power, performance, and area optimization.

  • RTL Coding for Synthesis
  • Timing Constraints & Optimization
  • Area & Power Optimization
  • Clock Tree Synthesis
  • Formal Verification
  • Low-Power Design Techniques

Static Timing Analysis (STA)

Master STA techniques to ensure timing closure in complex chip designs.

  • Timing Paths & Constraints
  • Setup & Hold Time Analysis
  • Clock Skew & Jitter Analysis
  • Crosstalk & Signal Integrity
  • STA Reports & Debugging
  • ECO Techniques

Physical Design & Verification

Learn advanced physical design techniques for modern chip implementation.

  • Floor planning & Power Planning
  • Placement & Optimization
  • Clock Tree Synthesis
  • Routing & DRC/LVS
  • Design for Testability (DFT)
  • Physical Verification & Signoff
