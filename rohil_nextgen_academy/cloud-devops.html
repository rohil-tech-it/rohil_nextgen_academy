DevOps Tools and Technologies

  • CI/CD Pipeline - Jenkins, GitLab CI, GitHub Actions

Frequently Asked Questions

How long is the DevOps course?

The course lasts for 80 hours, including hands-on labs, real-world projects, and capstone projects.

Do I need any prior knowledge before starting?

Basic knowledge of Linux and programming is helpful but not required. We start with fundamentals and progress to advanced topics.

Will I receive a certification?

Yes, you'll receive a recognized DevOps Engineer certification upon successful completion of the course and project work.

Will I learn DevOps tools?

Yes. Students will learn popular DevOps tools used in the industry including Docker, Kubernetes, Jenkins, Ansible, and Terraform.

What career paths can I explore after this course?

You can become a DevOps Engineer, Site Reliability Engineer (SRE), Cloud Engineer, Automation Engineer, or Release Manager.

Is job assistance provided?

Yes! We provide comprehensive job assistance including resume building, interview preparation, and placement support with our partner companies.
