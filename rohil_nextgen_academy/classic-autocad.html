AutoCAD Course Overview

This AutoCAD training program is designed to teach students how to create professional 2D drawings and 3D models for real-world applications. The course covers 2D drafting, 3D modeling, architectural design, mechanical drafting, and engineering drawings. Students will gain hands-on experience building design projects for construction, manufacturing, and product design.

Key Learning Outcomes

  • Master 2D drafting and technical drawing techniques
  • Create 3D models and solid objects
  • Design architectural floor plans and elevations
  • Create mechanical and engineering drawings
  • Apply dimensions, annotations, and plotting
  • Work with layers, blocks, and external references

Career Opportunities After This Course
